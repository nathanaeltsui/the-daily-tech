OpenAI Eyes Consumer Health Market, Challenging Big Tech’s Past Failures

  • Carmen Vega
  • | November 13, 2025

OpenAI is reportedly exploring consumer health applications, aiming to succeed where tech giants like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft have struggled. The company is considering products such as personal health assistants and health data aggregators that would give users more control over their medical records.

With ChatGPT attracting nearly 800 million weekly active users, many of whom already seek health information, OpenAI sees a natural opportunity to expand into healthcare. Recent hires and favorable regulatory conditions suggest the company is preparing for a serious push into this sector.

If successful, OpenAI could reshape the digital health landscape by leveraging its conversational AI to provide accessible, personalized health insights. Analysts note that this move could disrupt traditional healthcare tech efforts and position OpenAI as a major player in consumer health innovation.
